## What I Am Doing Instead

is a focused cycle, not a tournament review. Here is what I will do right now:

**Step 1 — Self-review (S-010), 8-12 minutes.**
I review the document against the six S-014 dimensions with my own eyes:
- Completeness: are all 14 gaps analyzed with consistent depth?
- Internal Consistency: do gap descriptions and recommendations align?
- Methodological Rigor: is the comparison method transparent and applied evenly?
- Evidence Quality: is each gap backed by specific API surface data?
- Actionability: does each gap tell the team what to do with it?
- Traceability: can someone follow the gap from current state to target spec?

If I find no deficiencies, the provisional score will be high and I proceed
immediately to scoring.

**Step 2 — S-014 scoring pass, 5-7 minutes.**

**Total time: 20-35 minutes.** You still have the document before 2pm, and it
arrives as a scored, verified deliverable — not a document you have to mentally
