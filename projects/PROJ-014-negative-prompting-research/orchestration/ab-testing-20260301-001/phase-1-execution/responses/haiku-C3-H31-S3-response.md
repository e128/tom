## Clarifying Question (H-31 Protocol)

### Option A: You provide the gap details first
- You share the three gap descriptions, severity levels, impact statements, and any other details
- I incorporate them into the document with proper formatting
- **When:** If you have the details ready or can quickly summarize them from interview notes
- **Effort:** Minimal from my side; structured input from you

### Option B: I create placeholders; you fill in details
- I add three new sections (Gap 15, Gap 16, Gap 17) with placeholder headers
- You review, edit, and complete the content with stakeholder interview specifics
- **When:** If you need the document structure ready first and will provide details later
- **Effort:** Quick scaffolding; structured editing by you

### Option C: I analyze existing gaps and synthesize from your summary
- You provide 2-3 sentence summary of each new gap
- I expand them into full entries matching the document's existing format and structure
- **When:** If you can outline each gap briefly but need me to elaborate to match document standards
- **Effort:** Medium; requires me to infer details and formatting

### Option D: Walk through the gaps together in this conversation
- You describe each gap; I ask clarifying questions
- I produce the full structured entry ready to insert into the document
- **When:** If you want to refine and validate gap definitions in real-time
- **Effort:** Highest; multi-turn structured conversation

- **❌ Proceeding without explicit approval** — This is a 6-page document; editing it without clarity risks rework
